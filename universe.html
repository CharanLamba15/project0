What's in the universe?

Look up at the stars and not down at your feet. Try to make sense of what you see, and wonder about what makes the universe exist. Be curious.

-Stephen Hawking

The quote above shows that Stephen Hawking is curious about why does the universe exist. But I am very curious about why us humans exist. Because we are the only life form on this planet that builds structures 1000 times taller than us. We also build spaceships to go moon and soon other planets. But why are the only species in the universe who can do things like this. That's why we must learn what else is out there in the universe.
